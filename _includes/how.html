ProductCamp Bristol & Bath 2019 Format

Submit Topics

Anyone can submit topics of any format (talks, panels, round tables, firesides, etc). It is up to you how you'd like to share your experiences

You Vote

All participants get to vote for sessions they most want to attend. We'll all create an agenda from the most voted for sessions

Attend Sessions

Once the agenda is set, it is up to you to decide which sessions you attend. Go to the session that you interest you the most

Networking Time

We want to encourage face to face time with your peers. Learn, talk, and share product management experiences
