Proud to be diverse

ProductCamp Bristol & Bath supports and encourages all people to attend and learn about product management. We are pleased that we are already a gender diverse group (check out ProductTankBristol).

In terms of neuro-diversity, we will provide a quiet "non-networking" space for anyone who needs five minutes to themselves - in particular people who struggle with networking, anxiety and sensory issues.

There is disabled access to all of the rooms. If you feel that we can do something to support you please let us know.
