ProductCamp Bristol & Bath 2019 Format

Create the agenda

In the morning, everyone will collaboratively create the agenda for the day. Either come prepared with something you would like to share (talk, workshop, roundtable etc) or put something together on the day.

Attend the sessions

You get to attend the sessions that interest you the most. If you want to move between sessions that's fine. And if you decide half way through the day that you want to run a session that's fine too.

Time to network

All through the day you will have a chance to meet and learn from other product people. And the organising team is always there for support should you need it.
